15.17 Commercial Harvest Established and Regulated.

(1) Purpose. The purpose of this section is to establish the regulations governing the harvest and registration of elk, the meat of which may be sold pursuant to this ordinance when subsequent sale or resale of the meat to non-members is contemplated.

(2) Effective Date.

(a) The provisions of this section shall not take effect until such time as the tribe's governing body specifically declares them effective by an enabling resolution.

(b) No member shall sell any elk meat pursuant to this section prior to the effective date as declared by the tribe's governing body in accordance with subs. (a).

(3) Commercial Harvest Generally. A member who, between July 1 and June 30, has lawfully harvested elk pursuant to this ordinance may register those elk for commercial purposes and may sell to any person only the whole or quartered carcasses (except for the heads, skins and hooves) of any of those elk, provided the member:

(a) complies with the commercial harvest provisions of this Section;

(b) complies with the terms and conditions of the enabling resolution of the tribe's governing body enacted pursuant to subs. (2)(a); and

(c) otherwise complies with the provisions of this ordinance applicable to elk hunting.

(4) Elk Commercial Harvest.

(a) A member may register pursuant to this subsection an elk lawfully harvested in any tribal elk management zone.

(b) When registering an elk under this subsection, a member shall present the entire elk carcass, whole or quartered for registration, and no registering official shall register an elk under this subsection unless the entire carcass, whole or quartered, is presented for registration.

(c) A member may register an elk under this subsection at any tribal registration station, or with any person authorized to register elk in the field, at which time the registering official shall affix a commercial harvest registration tag to the carcass.

(d) This subsection is not intended to limit a member's ability to otherwise register an elk under the provisions of Section 15.06 [General Elk Registration Provisions] for noncommercial purposes, but only shall serve to condition the harvest and limit the number of elk which the member may register under this subsection.

(e) Except as modified by the provisions of this subsection, the provisions of Section 15.05 [Elk Hunting Permits/Tags], including the requirements of Paragraph 5 of the Voigt Intertribal Task Force Protocol Noodamashkoozwe (Elk Harvest) Levels, pursuant to that Agreement, and of Section 15.06 [Elk Registration], shall apply to members desiring to register antlered elk under this subsection.

(5) Transfer of Entire Carcass Required. No member who has harvested and registered any antlered or antlerless elk pursuant to this section shall sell the elk without transferring the entire carcass, whole or quartered, except for the head, skin and hooves, the registration tag and such accompanying documents as the Tribe may require be transferred to the person to whom the elk is sold.

Tribal Hunting Hours in the 1837 and 1842 Ceded Territories of Wisconsin and Minnesota

    Great Lakes Indian Fish & Wildlife CommissionP.O. Box 9Odanah, WI 54861(715) 682-6619August 2019Hunting hours for big game and small game are provided below for the 1837 and 1842 ceded territories in Wisconsin (Table 1) and the 1837 ceded territory in Minnesota (Table 2). The listed times are all relative to local sunrise and sunset times. Local sunrise and sunset times can be determined using apps such as WEATHERBUG or SUN SURVEYOR LITE on a mobile device, or websites such as google.com or sunrise-sunset.org. Hunting hours for migratory birds are published annually in the federal register, and posted on GLIFWC's website (glifwc.org).Table 1.

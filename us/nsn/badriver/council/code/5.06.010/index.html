Bad River Band of the Lake Superior Chippewa Indians Tribal Code.

5.06.010 General Provisions

(a) Class III gaming may be conducted pursuant to this chapter at the following locations:

(1) The log building located on U.S. Hwy. 2, at SE¼ NW¼ Sec. 32, T48N R2W.

(2) Any other location designated by Tribal Council resolution.

(b) No person, natural or legal, or association of persons, other than the Tribe, may conduct Class III gaming on the reservation.

(c) All primary management officials and key employees of the Class III gaming operation shall be subject to the licensing provisions of Section 5.02.010(c).

(d) The chief management official of the Class III gaming operation shall be responsible for the development and implementation of policies to effectuate all standards for the conduct of games, prescribed by the Tribal Council including those policies and rules contained in Section 5.06.020 - 5.06.050, any tribal -state compact, and any National Indian Gaming Commission regulations.

(e) The chief management official of the Class III gaming operation shall be responsible for the conduct of all Class III gaming operations on the reservation and all Class II gaming operations conducted at Class III gaming facility.

(f) The chief management official of the Class III gaming operation shall be subject to the same powers and duties with respect to the Class III gaming operation as the Bingo Manager is with respect to Bingo operations.

(g) Any person, natural or legal, and any association thereof, contracting with the Tribe to provide management service to the Class III gaming operation, or gaming related goods or services exceeding in value $10,000 per year, shall be required to obtain a gaming contractor license from the tribe, to be issued by the tribal chairman only after an investigation as provided Section 5.02.010 (c) has been completed.

(h) No member of the Tribal Council, and no spouse, parent, child or sibling of a member of the Tribal Council, may have any direct or indirect financial interest in the Class III gaming operation, nor may any such person or any employee of the Class III gaming operation receive any payment, gift, or gratuity of any kind from any gaming contractor licensed by the Tribe or from any person, natural or legal, or any association thereof, engaged in the provision of gaming goods or services, whether or not such person or association is currently providing any such goods or services to the Tribe.

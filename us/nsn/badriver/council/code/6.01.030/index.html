Bad River Band of the Lake Superior Chippewa Indians Tribal Code.

6.01.030 Powers and Duties of Committee

The Committee shall have the following powers and duties:

(a) To maintain custody of all of the membership applications and supporting evidence, and all related documentation, correspondence and collateral records related to membership.

(b) To review applications for membership in the Tribe, and to make recommendations to the Tribal Council on such applications.

(c) To request and receive records of the Bureau of Indian Affairs to determine eligibility for membership.

(d) To recommend to the Tribal Council disenrollment as provided by this chapter.

(e) To advise the Tribal Council on matters related to membership and enrollment.

(f) To delegate to the enrollment clerk the ministerial duties of the Committee as the Committee chooses to delegate.

(g) All actions of the Committee are subject to review by the Tribal Council as provided herein, the decision of which shall in all matters be final and conclusive.

(12-5-90-154; 6-1-83-39; 8-13-79-161; 8-28-78-19.)
